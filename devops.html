Our Services

1. DevOps implementation consulting:

We provide DevOps consulting by helping companies implement the best DevOps practices from scratch and adapting them to and managing existing projects. We can help you leverage connected and automated development, testing, and deployment of your applications at any stage of your apps’ life cycle by:

  • Implementing the Infrastructure as Code (IaaC) methodology.
  • Adopting application module containerization.
  • Establishing continuous integration and deployment (CI/CD) pipelines.
  • Introducing automation to streamline testing processes.
  • Implementing automated application monitoring.

2. DevSecOps consulting:

Our DevOps consulting helps to create the best software and integrate security at all stages, effectively connecting people, processes, and technologies.

3. Project recovery consulting:

We offer the expertise of qualified consultants to elevate underdeveloped projects. Rapid issue resolution and tailored solutions ensure a swift transition, guiding your project toward successful deployment.

  • Uncover and address collaboration issues among development, testing, and operations teams.
  • Recognize and bridge knowledge gaps related to DevOps tools and methodologies.
  • Address technical challenges, such as configuration errors in CI/CD.
  • Enhance automated testing coverage and efficiency.
