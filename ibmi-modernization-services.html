Our Service


1.Modernize RPG

Our experts seamlessly convert ILE RPG code from fixed form to free form, simplifying your team's coding experience. Whether it's on-site or remote, we provide comprehensive support for application maintenance, bug fixes, enhancements, and predictive maintenance.

2. GUI on AS400/IBMi

Revitalize your user experience and operational efficiency by transforming outdated 5250 green screens into a modern graphical interface. Our tool-based approach, utilizing solutions like Profound, LANSA, Zend 5250 Bridge, and HTML/CSS web pages, breathes new life into your IBM i interface.

3. Tier Architecture

Rethink RPG language with our team of experts, transforming largely columnar code into a modern 3-tier architecture. We re-engineer code, improve coding standards, and ensure a future-ready architecture with integrity in multi-tier environments.

4. Database Modernization

Assess and modernize your DDS-based database, planning a seamless transition to DDL, the SQL twin of the DDS source. Our services include modern database access, custom conversion, tool-based conversion, and fast query processing.

5. Web & Mobile Access

Extend the accessibility of your IBM i machine to modern platforms with our services. Whether it's browser-based emulation, leveraging SOAP or RESTful interfaces, ensuring seamless integration, or maintaining source code integrity – we've got you covered.

6. Re-host on Cloud

Minimize costs and maintenance overheads by re-hosting your IBM machine on the cloud. Our non-invasive method ensures a quick win solution, using emulator tools for a secure and scalable transition without the need for extensive re-programming.
